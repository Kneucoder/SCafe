The Art of the Pour

How to do it!

This guide is designed to help you unlock the complex tasting notes of beans right in your kitchen.

"Coffee is not just a drink; it's a moment of clarity. Let's make it just right."

Brewing Guide

Follow the steps for artisanal excellence

01 water_drop

Heat your water

Bring fresh, filtered water to approximately 205°F (96°C). Avoid using boiling water directly to prevent scorching the delicate oils in the beans.

02 settings_input_component

Grind your beans

Use 20g of freshly roasted coffee. Grind to a medium-fine consistency, similar to kosher salt.

03 opacity

The Bloom

Pour 40g of water over the grounds. Let it sit for 30 seconds. This releases trapped CO2 and prepares the beans for extraction.

04 restart_alt

The Final Pour

Pour the remaining water in concentric circles, avoiding the edges. Aim for a total brew time of 3:00 minutes.
